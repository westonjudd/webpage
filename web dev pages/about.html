About Me

Hi name is Weston Judd! I am from Laguna Beach, California but currently live in Provo, Utah. I am an undergrad student at Brigham Young University studying business management.

Currently I work as a freelance photographer, specializing in film photography. In addition to my passion for photography, for the past 3 years I have worked as a risk manager for Live Bait LLC, a brokerage firm based out of San Diego, CA.

Beyond work and school, my hobbies include surfing, climbing, skiing, playing guitar, and camping!

Technical skills: CPR certified, Certified suicide crisis counselor, fluency in the Estonian language, SQL, VBA, HTML, Adobe Illustrator.
